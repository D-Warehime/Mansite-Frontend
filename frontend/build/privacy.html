Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

⚠️ SERVICE PURPOSE

Mansite is designed EXCLUSIVELY for sending SMS messages to YOUR OWN phone number.

This service is for personal entertainment and alibi creation only. You may NOT use this service to contact others.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Mansite, we collect:

  • Phone Numbers: The phone number you provide (must be your own)
  • Message Content: The text messages you send to yourself
  • IP Addresses: For security and abuse prevention
  • User Agent Information: For debugging and security
  • Consent Records: Proof of your agreement to terms
  • Usage Timestamps: When messages are sent
  • Compliance Data: Validation results and restrictions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver SMS Messages: Send messages to your own phone number
  • Ensure Compliance: Verify messages meet A2P 10DLC requirements
  • Prevent Abuse: Stop harassment, threats, or misuse
  • Maintain Security: Protect against fraud and violations
  • Legal Compliance: Meet SMS and privacy regulations
  • Service Improvement: Enhance functionality and reliability

3. Anti-Harassment and Legal Compliance

Mansite has a ZERO-TOLERANCE policy for harassment, threats, or misuse.

3.1 Data Monitoring

We actively monitor and analyze:

  • Message Content: For prohibited content (phone numbers, URLs, threats)
  • Usage Patterns: For unusual or suspicious activity
  • Phone Number Verification: To ensure self-use only
  • Compliance Violations: For A2P 10DLC requirements

3.2 Violation Detection

We automatically detect and prevent:

  • Harassment Attempts: Messages containing threats or abuse
  • Misuse Patterns: Attempts to contact others
  • Content Violations: Prohibited content in messages
  • Compliance Issues: Messages that violate SMS regulations

3.3 Legal Reporting

We will report to law enforcement:

  • Threats of Violence: Any threats of harm
  • Harassment: Repeated unwanted contact
  • Stalking: Unwanted following or monitoring
  • Cyberbullying: Online harassment or intimidation
  • Fraud: Attempts to deceive or defraud

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information:

  • With Twilio: Our SMS provider to send messages
  • With Law Enforcement: When required by law or for safety
  • For Legal Protection: To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With Carriers: To comply with SMS regulations
  • For Court Orders: When legally compelled to do so

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident Response: Procedures for security breaches
  • Data Backup: Secure backup and recovery systems

6. SMS Compliance and Regulations

We comply with all applicable SMS regulations:

  • TCPA Compliance: Telephone Consumer Protection Act
  • A2P 10DLC: Application-to-Person messaging standards
  • Carrier Requirements: Major mobile carrier guidelines
  • State Laws: Various state privacy and anti-harassment laws
  • International Standards: Where applicable

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for:

  • Service Provision: As long as necessary to provide the service
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements
  • Dispute Resolution: To resolve conflicts and enforce agreements
  • Security Monitoring: To prevent abuse and violations
  • Audit Purposes: For compliance and security reviews

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request copies of your information
  • Correct Information: Update inaccurate data
  • Delete Data: Request removal of your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop receiving SMS messages
  • Data Portability: Request your data in portable format
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the service entirely

9. Third-Party Services and Compliance

Our service uses these third-party providers:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery (see their privacy policy)
  • Netlify: Hosting and serverless functions
  • PostgreSQL: Secure data storage

All third-party services are vetted for security and compliance.

10. Age Restrictions and Protection

This service is NOT intended for users under 18 years old.

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children
  • Users must be 18+ to consent to SMS messages
  • Age verification may be required
  • Violations will result in immediate account termination

11. International Users and Data Transfer

This service is primarily designed for US users. International users should be aware:

  • Data is processed in the United States
  • Different privacy laws may apply in your country
  • SMS delivery may be subject to local regulations
  • You consent to data transfer to the US

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices
  • New legal requirements
  • Enhanced security measures
  • Improved compliance procedures

Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

13. Contact Information and Reporting

  • General Inquiries: Contact us through the main site
  • Privacy Questions: Email privacy@mansite.com (when available)
  • Violation Reports: Report immediately through the site

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: Your explicit agreement to receive SMS messages

  • Legal Obligation: To comply with SMS and privacy regulations
  • Legitimate Interest: To prevent abuse and ensure security
